2 Description of Service

ParkArmor is an iOS application that helps you remember where you parked your vehicle. Core features include:

  • Parking location saving — Captures your current GPS coordinates and saves them as a parking record on your device
  • Navigation assistance — Provides walking directions back to your parked vehicle via Apple Maps
  • Parking timer — Allows you to set a countdown timer with a local notification reminder before your parking expires
  • Parking photos — Lets you photograph your parking spot or signage for reference
  • Parking history — Maintains a local log of past parking locations
  • Home Screen widget — Displays your active parking location on the iOS Home Screen (ParkArmor Pro)

ParkArmor is designed for iOS and requires iOS 17.0 or later. The App does not require an account or internet connection to function.

5 Data Collection & Usage

All data is stored locally on your device. ParkArmor does not have a backend server. We do not transmit, upload, or share your personal data with any third-party server or service. Your data never leaves your iPhone.

Location Data

ParkArmor requests access to your device's location services exclusively to capture the GPS coordinates of your current position when you tap "Park Here." This data is:

  • Stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Used only to display your parking location on the map and calculate walking directions back to your vehicle
  • Never transmitted to any server or shared with any third party
  • Never accessed in the background after the App is closed (location is requested once, at the moment you save a parking spot)

You may revoke location permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → ParkArmor.

Photos

If you choose to photograph your parking spot or signage, those photos are:

  • Stored locally on your device, linked to the corresponding parking record
  • Compressed to reduce storage footprint before saving
  • Never uploaded to any server or shared externally
  • Permanently deleted when you delete the associated parking record

Camera and photo library access requires your permission and can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings.

Notifications

ParkArmor uses local push notifications (scheduled on your device — not sent from a server) to remind you when a parking timer is about to expire. We do not use remote push notifications. Notification permission can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings → Notifications → ParkArmor.

Purchase Status

Your ParkArmor Pro purchase status is stored in a shared iOS App Group container on your device so the Home Screen widget can access it. This data is never transmitted externally.

No Analytics or Tracking

ParkArmor does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your usage, collect device identifiers, or profile your behavior.
